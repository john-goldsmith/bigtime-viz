# BigTime Viz

## Running Locally
1. `npm i`
1. `npm start`

## Building
1. `npm run build`

## Ideas
* Time ranges:
  * Week
  * 1 month
  * 3 months
  * 6 months
  * 1 year
  * Custom
* Person [Gantt chart](http://layer0.authentise.com/gantt-chart-with-reactjs-and-d3js.html):
  * Y-axis: projects
  * X-axis: time
* Pie chart:
  * Percentage of time by project
* Line chart:
  * Total hours logged across all people and all projects
    * Y-axis: hours
    * X-axis: time
  * Total hours logged by project
    * Y-axis: hours
    * X-axis: time
* Bar chart:
  * Total hours logged by all people
    * Y-axis: hours
    * X-axis: people
